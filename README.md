# 3D Personality Plotter

Last modified: On an auspicious day in June, 2026 ☕

A visual analytical tool that maps human behavioral tendencies and personality traits onto a three-dimensional coordinate space.

This tool was born from a deeply personal motivation: **the developer's own struggle with human relationships.** It was designed as a self-debugging instrument — a way to step back and observe one's own patterns of interaction with some degree of objectivity, in the hope of building slightly better connections with others.

---

## 🎯 Intended Use Cases

- Personal introspection, self-understanding, and behavioral self-debugging
- Objective structural analysis of historical figures, literary characters, or political ideologies
- Academic research and education in psychology, behavioral ecology, and organizational theory

> **Note:** Commercial use such as 💰 marketing is not recommended, and I would prefer that this tool not be used for such purposes.

---

## 🚀 How to Use

The tool currently displays in Japanese only, and the sample data is also based primarily on Japanese cases. For this reason, I recommend reading the Japanese instructions on the tool page.
